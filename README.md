I developed a Soliris patient-identification and activity framework that used linked administrative claims and EHR data to identify treated patients across PNH, aHUS, and gMG and to assemble a unified view of diagnosis, treatment, dose-related activity, and patient characteristics. The goal was to pull market-relevant Soliris exposure from medical claims, pharmacy claims, EHR medication administration, EHR procedures, and EHR prescription records; assign members to their primary administrative diagnosis; reconcile overlapping treatment evidence across sources; and produce analyzable patient-level datasets that supported rare-disease treatment profiling and utilization review.
